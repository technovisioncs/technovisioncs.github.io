Registration Amount : 20/-

Rules

  • The game will be of 2 vs 2 players.
  • There will be two ring on both side of players.
  • The first player of each team will throw the mail into the ring.
  • The second player will run and complete the given task written in the mail.
  • Once the task is completed, the team has to shout -‘Mail me’.
  • The pair which finishes its task first will qualify to the next round.
  • Each slot will have 3 rounds.
  • Any kind of misbehaviour, the participant will be disqualified.
