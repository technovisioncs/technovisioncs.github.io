Introduction

Being one of the biggest event of Technovision and one of its kind , Code Mania will not only test your programming skills but your passion towards programming.
It would be the ultimate test of your concentration skills with each round of it .There will be three round in total in which each round code will get difficult. Do you think you are a good programmer ? Come test your skills .
Entry fee : 20/-

Rounds

Round 1: It would be simple debugging test. Where you will be given two programs in which you just need to debug the errors.

Round 2: This round would be difficult then previous one. In this round you have to debug errors as well as correct the logic.

Round 3:This round would be little tricky. Here you will be given question and you have to type the whole code with screen turned off. Participants with least error will be announced winner.

Rules

  • You can choose either C++ or Java language only .
  • Time limit will be provided in each round accordingly.
  • Participants are not allowed to bring any media devices such as pen drives, CD, DVD etc.
  • Use of cell phones is not allowed.
  • Prize money will be transferred to your respective bank account.
