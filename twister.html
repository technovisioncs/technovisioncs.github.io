Rules

  • Only two participants will be entertained.
  • Help from people who are not participating in the event should not be taken.

  • Clues must be collected in proper order.

  • Flag is compulsory at the end point.
  • In case of loss of any clues the team is directly disqualified
  • If caught cheating, by volunteer the team will be disqualified.

  • No permission to walk on grass area.
  • Same colour clues must be taken.
  • Prize money will be transferred online (RTGS).
